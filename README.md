# HooshTeb

**HooshTeb** is an AI-powered medical intake platform designed for clinics and medical offices.

The platform allows patients to complete their medical history, describe their symptoms, and provide the reason for their visit before meeting the physician. This helps reduce waiting time, improves the quality of consultations, and enables physicians to review patient information in advance.

## Features

* Patient pre-visit medical history collection
* Symptom and complaint registration
* AI-assisted organization of patient information
* Faster and more efficient clinical workflow
* Responsive web interface

## Vision

Our goal is to simplify the patient intake process and enhance communication between patients and healthcare professionals by leveraging modern AI technologies.
